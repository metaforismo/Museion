# Landing V2

The landing makes the product method tangible before explaining it. Its hero pairs one precise thesis with a real deterministic algebra micro-interaction: source evidence, learner commitment, local feedback, and Maia’s follow-up. The page then distinguishes model pedagogy from deterministic authority, visualizes the learning cycle, introduces Maia, states evidence boundaries, and links to the keyless lesson/library.

The experience is intentionally not a generic AI hero: no chat-first empty box, fake metrics, glowing gradients, or unsupported outcome claims.
